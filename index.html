Biscuits and Gravy Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 2tbs butter
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tsp pepper (or too taste)
  • 1 lb sausage

I like to use italian sausage, but you might need to use less pepper depending on how much you want.

Cooking Process:

  1. Set to medium high heat and add oil to pan
  2. Put in sausage, break with wooden spoon, and cook untill there is no pink
  3. Add flour and butter (mix in between)
  4. After butter melts, add half of the milk a little at a time (mix throughout all process and going forward)
  5. Add more milk after it thickens, but keep a little milk
  6. Add some pepper and salt to taste
  7. Add rest of milk
  8. Add more pepper if needed
  9. After it thickens, bring heat to medium low, then low

This is the video I got this recipe from.

I slightly modified it by using Italian sausage, which gives it more of a peppery taste.
